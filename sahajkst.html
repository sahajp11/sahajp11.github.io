Visit Singapore!

 
 
                       

    Singapore is a small island city state (country) that lies just above the equator in southeast Asia. Singapore is considered to be the most richest, cleanest, smartest, and safest country in the world. Also, it is one of the most diverse. The main ethnicities are Indian, Chinese, Malay, and Eurasian. The national languages of Singapore are English, Chinese, Malay, and Tamil. The currency used is the Singapore Dollar.

With many places to visit, many foods to try, and activities to do, Singapore is the perfect utopia!
